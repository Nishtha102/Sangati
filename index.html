FAQ

Questions families ask before joining.

How do online classes work?

You receive a class link, join live from home, and follow clear verbal and visual guidance.

Can senior citizens join?

Yes. Senior batches use slower transitions, chair options, and joint-friendly movements.

Do I need prior experience?

No. Beginners can start with trial classes or a personal consultation.

What equipment is required?

A yoga mat, water bottle, comfortable clothing, and optional chair or cushion are enough.

How are one-on-one classes conducted?

Private video sessions include assessment, customized routines, and progress follow-up.

What is included in the fitness course?

Yoga, mobility, strength, pranayama, meditation, nutrition guidance, tracking, recordings, and certification.
